Apartment 1

The apartments are equipped with new furniture. In addition to seating furniture in the living room and a french bed in the bedroom, guests can count on a stove, microwave, toaster, plates, dishes, cutlery. Guests can use WiFi, but there are also cable TV programs on modern LCD. Object is designed so that it does not violate the mountain environment, characterized by a very high quality construction. As a result, the apartments are in the winter, very warm, and, in summer, a very pleasant stay. Guests can themselves regulate the temperature in the apartments.

The apartments have a terrace, where you can spend romantic moments. Accommodation for 2 + 2 people, but if you get more guests, it is possible to rent more apartments. If necessary, guests will be provided cots for bebe. For guests are provided parking spaces in the underground garage.
